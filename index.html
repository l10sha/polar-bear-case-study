A case study by Polar Bear

A people growth ecosystem
built for a digital agency
that's scaling on talent.

Through our approach and orchestration, leadership gained a shared, structured view of skills, growth, and potential across 35 people on the team.

Digital CX Agency
50 people  US · Brazil · India

What we did

Built a career framework

A behavior-driven framework aligned with the agency's strategy. Where it stands today and where it's heading.

Created a transparent skills map

Full visibility on skills coverage, growth areas, aspirations, and placement fit across the whole team.

Ran end-to-end review cycle

The full cycle: self-reflections, feedback collection, calibration committees, and final delivery to every employee.

Built leadership capabilities

Trained the team on self-reflection writing, feedback collection, and coached advisors on how to deliver it well.

Created internal ownership

Equipped one team member to own and run the next cycle independently. The process is now fully theirs.

Key outcomes

Full team picture

Leadership gained visibility on skills, growth areas, and aspirations across every person.

Patterns surfaced

Recurring development needs emerged clearly, shaping how the agency will invest in its people going forward.

Smarter placement decisions

A few role mismatches were identified. Leadership now has the context to explore lateral moves that serve both person and team.

Capability gaps made visible

Skill gaps in the agency's offering became concrete, informing hiring and development priorities.

A baseline for what comes next

This cycle is the foundation. Every future review builds on it. They're not starting from scratch again.

AI-powered solution simplifying review cycle orchestration to enable better human conversations.
